Your Surface Plates Are Not Flat

To be sure that they are at least flat enough, they can be measured with angle measurement devices such as autocollimators and repeat-o-meters to generate maps of your measurment surfaces.

You may hire third parties to perform this work, or do it yourself with this handy tool and your own autocollimator. Read up on the original Moody Method and instructions, then make sure to follow this tool to get the most reliable results possible.



This procedure and documentation was created with constant reference to ASME B89.3.7-2013, and any tolerances or decision rules bandied about are directly from that document.

Values pre-populated in the Tool are for demonstration purposes. Replace them with your own values.
